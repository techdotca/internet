Internet Deals

MAY 2020

It is no news that Canadians are paying exhorbitant prices for internet. We pay one of the highest prices in the developed world. Tech Dot compiled a list of the best internet deals currently available.

1. Carrytel

Carrytel currently has an ongoing promotion for 50 mbps for 30$. They are relatively new as they launched a few years ago, however, their service is pretty solid and decent. Their customer support is also above average.

Pros: Free installation, Additional 10$ discount using this promo code: HO79427 or RA9563
Cons: Modem rental deposit (29$), Older modem/router

2. Fizz

Pros: High reliability, Free installation, Up-to-date modem/router
Cons: On the pricier side

3. Teksavvy

Teksavvy has been active for more than 20 years. Their customer support is one of the best.

Pros: Unlimited usage from 2AM to 6A
Cons: On the pricier side

4. CIK

CIK has been in the business for many years now. They offer cheap prices. However, they are known to throttle your speed.

Pros: Low cost
Cons: Throttling
